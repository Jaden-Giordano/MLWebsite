Custom Enchantments

An Original MineLegends Experience

Custom Enchants has been in development for a long time, and is one of MineLegends' most prominent features. We are extremely excited to see what you guys think! *hint* IT ROCKS!!

Enchanments

  • Harvest

    Enchant Type: Weapon
    Max Level: III

    Description: On death, have a chance of dropping the enemies head.

    Leveling:
    I: 25% chance of dropping head
    II: 35% chance of dropping head
    III: 45% chance of dropping head (5% chance of insta-killing a player within 5 blocks)

  • Infernal

    Enchant Type: Armor
    Max Level: II

    Description: On hit have a chance of spawning blazes to battle your attacker.

    Leveling:
    I: 25% chance of spawning blazes
    II: 45% chance of spawning blazes

  • Purification

    Enchant Type: Armor
    Max Level: III

    Description: On hit have a chance of removing all potion effects and restoring half health.

    Leveling:
    I: 15% chance of removing effects
    II: 20% chance of removing effects (5% chance of moving negatives to enemy)
    III: 35% chance of removing effects (10% chance of moving negatives to enemy)

  • Surge

    Enchant Type: Weapon
    Max Level: II

    Description: When equipped get an extra row of hearts.

    Leveling:
    No leveling....

  • Trap

    Enchant Type: Chestplate
    Max Level: II

    Description: On hit have a chance of removing your enemies ability to walk and see for 2-3 seconds.

    Leveling:
    I: 15% chance of flinging enemy
    II: 20% chance of flinging enemy

  • Obliterate

    Enchant Type: Fishing Rod
    Max Level: II

    Description: On hit have a chance of blasting your enemy into the sky pulling the ground out from under them.

    Leveling:
    I: 15% chance of shooting your enemy from the ground, flinging them and the ground beneath high into the air.
    II: 25% chance of shooting your enemy from the ground, flinging them and the ground beneath high into the air.
    III: 30% chance of shooting your enemy from the ground, flinging them and the ground beneath high into the air.
